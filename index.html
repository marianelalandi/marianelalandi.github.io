I am Marianela Landi

International student-athlete from Misiones, Argentina, pursuing my Masters of Science in Data Analytics at the University of Illinois and working for the Illinois Department of Revenue, inside the Department of Innovation and Technology as a System Analyst and for the Univeristy of Illinois as a Reseach Assistant in Data Analytics. Graduated from the College of Coastal Georgia with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and minors in Economics and Data Analytics in May 2022. Planning on getting my Masters degree in May 2024 and work as a Data Analyst in the US.

I personally enjoy applying my coding skills to extract meaningful insights from data that is difficult to achieve using conventional inspection methods, and creating frameworks to identify or predict patterns in data. I believe that transforming raw data to actionable insights is the key to empower a company, to not only solve problems but also to thrive, innovate, and adapt in a dynamic business environment.
